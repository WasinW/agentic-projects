# Content & Channels — LUMORA

> Content strategy, channel mix, brand definition, and guardrails for the **LUMORA** project — an AI-art creator business spanning many catalogs (สายมู, อาหาร/สุขภาพ, ท่องเที่ยว, gadget, art…). The strategy is **catalog-agnostic**; this file uses Thai spiritual/mystical (สายมู) as **catalog #1** — the worked example. Pairs with `01_creative_library.md` (the axes + library), `05_multi_account.md` (per-account split), and `04_tech_backend.md`. Operating principle from Sin: **diverge → keep diverging → combine selectively**.
>
> **v3 update:** the per-account unifier is no longer just "voice" — it is now grounded as **Brand Archetype + Audience Persona + Niche scope** (see §0). Voice/tone is *expressed* through the archetype; the archetype is what's fixed. Pillars are now a **selection of Content axis codes (C1–C10)** per account, not a global fixed 4.

## 0. Account-Level Definition (fix ครั้งเดียวต่อ account)

> The **account-level layer** — set once per account, then never whiplash. Everything post-level (pillars, theme, media, tone) varies *under* this fixed frame. This replaces the old "voice is the only unifier": the unifier is now **archetype + persona + niche**; voice is how that archetype talks. Cross-ref `01_creative_library.md` §7 and `05_multi_account.md` (one block *per account*).

### 0.1 Brand Archetype (Jung 12)

Pick **1 primary** (optionally 1 secondary) from the 12 Jungian archetypes. (Archetype choice is per-account and per-catalog; the four below are the natural fits for **catalog #1, สายมู** — another catalog would pick its own.) For สายมู, four fit naturally:

| Archetype | สายมู fit | Voice it produces |
|---|---|---|
| 🪄 **Magician** | Transformation, ritual, "เปลี่ยนพลังงาน/โชค" — core มู energy | Mystical, transformative, "เปิดประตูอีกโลก" |
| 🦉 **Sage** | Knowledge of tradition, คาถา/ตำนาน, explain-the-meaning | Wise, contemplative, teacher-but-humble |
| 🧭 **Explorer** | "Fellow explorer of spiritual aesthetics" — Sin's core stance | Curious, journeying, "ไปลองดูด้วยกัน" |
| 🃏 **Jester** | มูLife comedy, self-deprecating relatability | Playful, irreverent-about-self, viral |

> Magician/Sage/Explorer/Jester are the natural fits — but **one account = one primary archetype** (a Sage account and a Jester account should usually be *different* accounts; cross-ref `05_multi_account.md`).

### 0.2 Audience Persona (1–2 per account)

Write 1–2 concrete personas — age, location, relationship to มู, what they scroll for. Example:

> **"GenZ มู สาย aesthetic"** — 22–32, Bangkok working pro, เชื่อแบบ aesthetic + personal-meaning (ไม่ใช่ traditionalist เคร่ง), เสพ art/anime, ช้อป TikTok Shop, อยากให้ความเชื่อตัวเอง "ดู cool." Hires the feed to *feel calm + feel cool about believing*.

A second persona can widen reach (e.g. "สาย traditional มู 30–45, ขอพรจริงจัง, AOV สูงกว่าแต่ conservative กว่า") — but if two personas pull the aesthetic in opposite directions, that's a signal to **split into two accounts**, not stretch one.

### 0.3 Niche Scope

Set **broad vs narrow** per account. Broad (all of สายมู) = bigger ceiling, slower algo clarity. Narrow (e.g. "oracle only" or "cyber-spiritual deities only") = faster algo lock, smaller pool. Sequential-expansion plan (`05`) usually starts **one broad-ish account → validate → spin narrower accounts** off the winning pockets.

### Pillar notes

- **Pillar 1** is the engine — it feeds the algorithm daily and is the most automatable (AI pipeline → 30–50 images/day → pick best). Treat misses as cheap.
- **Pillar 2** is the *retention* pillar — "มาดูทุกเช้า" behavior. Same slot, same time daily = a ritual. This is where subscription value lives later.
- **Pillar 3** is the *reach* pillar — comedy travels furthest on TikTok. Lowest direct revenue, highest follower velocity. This is where the brand gets *human*.
- **Pillar 4** is the *trust + monetization* pillar — real face/feet on the ground at real ที่ไหว้, where affiliate (hotel, ร้านอาหาร, เครื่องราง nearby) converts and "sincerity" gets proven.

**Balance rule:** Pillars 1–2 carry volume (~70% of posts), 3–4 carry differentiation. Never let the feed become all-AI-image — the comedy + real travel are what stop it feeling like a soulless prompt farm.

## 1b. Optional Per-Post Lenses — JTBD + HHH

> Two **optional** lenses applied *per post* on top of the pillar. Full theory lives in `01_creative_library.md`; here is just how they shape the actual content choice.

### JTBD (Jobs To Be Done)

Ask: *what job does the viewer "hire" this post to do?* The answer steers tone + format. Tag format: **"When [situation], help me [outcome] so I can [reason]."**

| Job the viewer hires | Shapes the post toward |
|---|---|
| "Help me feel calm before work" | Contemplative tone (T1), single atmospheric image |
| "Help me feel cool about my beliefs" | Strong aesthetic, cyber/cosmic theme |
| "Help me laugh at myself" | Comedy skit (C9, T2) |
| "Help me know what to buy" | Product-forward, affiliate angle |
| "Help me find sacred sites" | Travelogue (C4), real footage |

### HHH (Hero / Hub / Hygiene) cadence

Production-cadence lens (Google/YouTube model). Targets the *mix*, not every post:

| Stage | Share | Role | Effort |
|---|---|---|---|
| 🦸 **Hero** | **5%** | Tentpole / viral push / brand awareness | High, rare |
| 🔁 **Hub** | **35%** | Recurring series (oracle, มูLife) — retention | Medium |
| 🧹 **Hygiene** | **60%** | Evergreen / search / always-on daily art | High volume |

> Rule of thumb: the daily-art engine (Pillar 1) is mostly **Hygiene**; the oracle + comedy series are **Hub**; a big festival/seasonal drop is **Hero**. Skippable in early Phase 1 — turn it on once volume is steady.

## 2. Channel Strategy

### Priority (Phase 1, first 6 months)

> **Per-account** priority — each account sets its own channel weighting. The default below fits an art/reel-forward account; a travelogue-heavy account may lean more long-form YouTube, a comedy account more TikTok.

| Channel | Effort | Role | % focus |
|---|---|---|---|
| **TikTok + IG Reels** | High | Primary — algorithm pushes new creators hardest here | **70%** |
| **YouTube Shorts** | ~0 extra | Repurpose the same vertical asset | (free) |
| **LinkedIn** | Low | Repurpose text, build B2B authority for Phase 2 | **10%** |
| **Long-form YouTube** | Medium | 1/month deep-dive, SEO + evergreen | **20%** |

### Multi-Platform Linkage (how the cross-posting wiring actually works)

- **IG + Meta are linked** — publishing a Reel auto-cross-posts across the Meta family (IG ↔ FB Reels surface). One publish, two Meta surfaces, no extra effort.
- **TikTok is separate** — must be posted natively (own caption, own sounds, own hashtags). Do not just dump a watermarked TikTok export to IG; re-export clean.
- **YouTube (Shorts + long-form) is separate** — its own upload, its own metadata.

> Practical wiring: produce one 9:16 master → (1) native TikTok upload, (2) IG Reel which fans out to Meta automatically, (3) YouTube Shorts upload. Three publishes cover four+ surfaces.

### Skip until audience is built — and why

| Skip | Why (new-creator algorithm reality) |
|---|---|
| **Facebook page** | Organic reach is effectively dead for new pages; pay-to-play. Wrong place to build from zero. |
| **IG feed (static grid)** | IG now favors Reels for discovery. Posting to the grid spends effort the algorithm won't reward. Use **Reels only**. |
| **Twitter/X** | Low ROI for *visual* content in TH market; text-first network, weak for a สายมู art brand. |

> **Principle:** a new creator wins by going *deep* on the 1–2 surfaces where the algorithm actively hands out free reach (short-form video), not by spraying thin across 6 platforms. Pick the format the algorithm is subsidizing, master it, repurpose everywhere downstream.

### Why TikTok-first specifically

- Cold-start reach for new accounts is the best of any platform — content can outrank follower count.
- **TikTok Shop ของมู** = one of the highest-converting affiliate categories in TH (5–15% commission), so reach maps directly to revenue.
- IG Reels consumes the *same* vertical asset → two primary surfaces, one production cost.

## 3. Posting & Batching Discipline

### Batch by aesthetic, not by day

The creative library can produce 8000+ combinations — but **flipping aesthetics post-to-post confuses the algorithm and the audience**. The recommendation engine reads visual consistency as a signal of "who to show this to."

**Solution — batch by aesthetic in week-long blocks:**

```
Week 1:  Cyber-spiritual (A2)   → neon/atmospheric run
Week 2:  Traditional Thai (A1)  → gold/red/ornate run
Week 3:  Ghibli/pastoral (A8)   → soft nature run
Week 4:  Comedy-forward (A3+T2) → anime b-roll skit run
```

- Within a week, vary **subject, format, tone** freely — keep the **look** coherent.
- Each aesthetic block trains the algorithm on a clean audience pocket, then you rotate to surface the next pocket. Over a month the account reaches *multiple* communities without whiplashing any single one.
- This also makes production efficient: one prompt/style locked = batch-generate, batch-shoot, batch-edit.

### Archetype consistency > aesthetic consistency

> The feed can look like five different artists, but it must read as **one archetype + persona** (§0). Aesthetic is the wardrobe; archetype/voice is the personality — and audiences follow the personality, not the wardrobe. (If the aesthetics truly can't share one archetype, that's a multi-account signal — §6 + `05`.)

### Cadence for a new account

- **Post daily** in the first 90 days — frequency is the fastest lever for cold-start growth; the algorithm needs reps to learn who you are.
- Consistency of *posting time* matters for Pillar 2 (oracle) — same morning slot = ritual.
- Batching (above) is what makes daily posting *survivable* while holding a job. Generate/shoot in bulk, schedule out.

## 4. Voice / Positioning

> Voice is **how the §0 Brand Archetype talks.** The "fellow explorer" stance below is the natural voice of the **Explorer** archetype (with Magician/Sage/Jester shading per account). Set the archetype + persona first (§0), then the voice falls out of it — these draft lines are the Explorer-archetype default.

### Core stance

> **Fellow explorer, not guru.** Thai mystical roots × global art language. Sometimes contemplative, sometimes funny, always exploring.

**Identity:** A curious traveler of spiritual aesthetics — not a fortune-teller, not a comedian, both and neither. Tech is invisible craft, never the subject.

### Why "fellow explorer" is the safe + strong frame

- **Safer:** no authority claims → no liability for predictions/outcomes, no backlash for "ทำตัวเป็นผู้รู้."
- **Stronger:** the audience joins a journey *with* you rather than receiving decrees *from* you — higher para-social warmth, more shareable, more honest.

### Brand voice — draft lines

Long form:
> "Spiritual aesthetic explorer — Thai mystical roots × global art language. Mixing tech, tradition, and tone. Sometimes contemplative, sometimes funny, always exploring."

Short form (TH):
> "เล่าเรื่องมูผ่านศิลปะหลายภาษา"

Sample caption voices (test these on real posts):
- **Contemplative:** "บางวันท่านท้าวเวสฯ ก็อยู่ใน Neo-Bangkok นะ — เราแค่เปลี่ยนภาษาที่เล่า แต่ความเคารพเท่าเดิม"
- **Playful:** "ขอแล้วยังไม่ได้? อาจไม่ใช่ท่านไม่ให้ — อาจเป็นเราที่ยังไม่ไปต่อ 😌"
- **Comedy (self-deprecating):** "ประเภทคนเข้าวัด: คนขอครบทุกเรื่องใน 1 ธูป (เรานี่แหละ)"
- **Reverent:** "คาถาบทนี้คนโบราณท่องมาเป็นร้อยปี — วันนี้ขอวาดให้มันสวยพอที่จะส่งต่อ"

> **Voice test:** read any caption without the image. If you can tell it's *this* account from the words alone, the voice is doing its job.

## 5. Short-Form Mechanics

Where the craft of the format lives. Aesthetic gets the scroll to *pause*; mechanics get it to *stay, loop, and convert*.

### Hook (first 1–2 seconds)

- The first frame + first line decide 80% of retention. Lead with the **most striking visual or the sharpest line**, never a slow build or a logo intro.
- Hook patterns that work for this niche:
  - **Visual shock** — "ท่านท้าวเวสฯ แบบ cyberpunk" full-frame on frame 1.
  - **Open loop** — "ของมูชิ้นนี้คนเข้าใจผิดกันมากที่สุด…"
  - **Relatable jab (comedy)** — "ประเภทคนเข้าวัดที่ทุกคนรู้จัก…"
- No "สวัสดีครับวันนี้จะมา…" — cut it. Start in the middle of the value.

### Retention + loop

- **Seamless loop** (last frame → first frame) for art reels inflates watch-time → more reach. Design Pillar 1 reels to loop.
- One idea per video. Confusion = swipe.
- Captions/text-on-screen for sound-off viewing + accessibility; many watch muted.
- Pacing: a cut or beat change every 1–2s holds attention on TikTok.

> Posting cadence (daily / 90 days / batched, same oracle time-slot) lives in §3 — don't chase one viral spike, the algorithm rewards *sustained* output over one hit.

### Hashtags — one per dimension

Build a **hashtag library keyed to the creative-library dimensions** so tagging is systematic, not guesswork. ~3–5 per post:

- **Subject:** #ท้าวเวสสุวรรณ #เจ้าแม่กวนอิม #ยันต์ #สายมู
- **Aesthetic:** #cyberpunk #aiart #animeart #ghibli
- **Tone/format:** #สายมูสายอาร์ต #มูเตลู #oracle #dailycard
- **Reach/broad:** #สายมู #ของมู #fyp

> Mix one broad-reach tag + niche-specific tags so the post is legible to both the wide pool and the exact community.

### Repurposing flow — 1 asset → many channels

```
AI art / reel asset (vertical 9:16)
   │
   ├──► TikTok            (primary post, native caption + sounds)
   ├──► IG Reels          (same vertical, IG-native hashtags)
   ├──► YouTube Shorts    (same vertical, zero extra edit)
   │
   ├──► (monthly) bundle 4–6 assets ──► Long-form YouTube  (deep-dive / process / story)
   └──► (text take)               ──► LinkedIn  (the "building in public / AI craft" angle for B2B)
```

One production effort → 3 short-form surfaces same-day, plus a monthly long-form compilation and a LinkedIn text repurpose. **Produce once, distribute five ways.**

## 6. ⚠️ Warnings & Guardrails (v3 — 10 items)

> These guardrails are written for **catalog #1 (สายมู)**, where cultural/sacred sensitivity is highest. The platform/IP gates (7, 9, 10) apply to every catalog; the sincerity/sacred-imagery gates (1–4, 8) are catalog-specific — each new catalog should restate its own equivalent (e.g. health claims for อาหาร/สุขภาพ).

The สายมู audience detects insincerity fast, and the platforms themselves are shifting. These are non-negotiable gates, not style preferences. Items 1–6 are the cultural/voice gates; **items 7–10 are the new v3 platform/cultural-risk additions.**

| # | Guardrail | Rule |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Respect > satire** | Comedy must be **self-deprecating, never mocking**. Joke about *yourself* as a มู person ("เรานี่แหละ"), never about believers, beliefs, or sacred figures. Crossing into เยาะเย้ย = heavy, fast backlash. |
| 2 | **AI art + sacred imagery (homage)** | Treat as **respectful homage, not casual remix.** Some audiences are sensitive to AI-generated เทพ/ยันต์. Keep proportions/symbols dignified; avoid distortion or "for the lolz" mashups of sacred figures. When unsure, err reverent. |
| 3 | **Don't be a guru** | "**Fellow explorer**" framing only. No authority posture, no "ผู้รู้." You make art and explore — you don't dispense verdicts. |
| 4 | **No prediction / medical claims** | **AVOID** "ทำแล้วเลื่อนตำแหน่ง 100%," "บูชาแล้วหายป่วย," guaranteed outcomes. **Scammy + legally risky** (consumer-protection / false-advertising). Frame as belief/aesthetic/personal meaning — never a deterministic promise or health advice. |
| 5 | **Cross-aesthetic balance** | Don't whiplash aesthetics post-to-post (§3). Batch by week. Variety is a strength only when *sequenced*, not scrambled. **Multi-account is the structural fix** (see note below + `05_multi_account.md`). |
| 6 | **Voice / archetype consistency** | Across every aesthetic and tone, the account stays its **one archetype + persona** (§0). Drift = the account reads as a content farm. |
| 7 | **TikTok Shop commission ลด (2025)** | Commission rates were cut in 2025 — **diversify, don't rely on one platform's affiliate economics.** Spread monetization (digital products, other affiliate networks, brand deals) so a single platform's policy change can't gut revenue. |
| 8 | **AI image of *real* deities** | Generating real, worshipped เทพเจ้า via AI is **culturally sensitive** — some viewers find AI depiction of sacred figures disrespectful regardless of intent. **Watch reactions closely**, lead with reverence, and be ready to pull a piece. Higher caution than generic mystical art. |
| 9 | **Algorithm shadow-ban on "religion"** | Some platforms quietly **suppress reach on religious/"religion"-flagged content.** Test carefully — vary how spiritual content is framed/tagged, watch for reach cliffs, and don't lean the entire account on a single phrasing the algo may be throttling. |
| 10 | **The1 IP boundary** | Pattern/architecture knowledge is fine to reuse; **no proprietary code, data, or internal specifics** from the day job. Keep the creator business cleanly separate. |

### Multi-aesthetic → multi-account (the structural fix for §5)

> Sin likes **multiple aesthetics**, not one — and forcing them onto a single feed is exactly what triggers the cross-aesthetic confusion in Warning §5. The clean fix isn't to suppress the range; it's to **split aesthetics across accounts** (each account = one coherent archetype + persona + aesthetic family). Within an account, batch by week; across accounts, let the aesthetics diverge fully. See `05_multi_account.md` for the split patterns + sequential-expansion path.

### Quick gut-check before any post

- Would a sincere มู person feel **respected** or **made fun of**? (Must be respected.)
- Does this **promise an outcome**? (If yes — rewrite as meaning/belief, not guarantee.)
- Am I posing as **an authority**? (If yes — soften to explorer.)
- Is this a **real worshipped deity rendered by AI**? (If yes — extra reverence, watch reactions.)
- Does it **fit this account's archetype + this week's aesthetic block**? (If no — hold, or it belongs on a different account.)
- Could I tell it's *this account* from the caption alone? (If no — strengthen the voice.)

Reach is the aesthetic; trust is the archetype; durability is multi-account + diversified monetization; revenue is the guardrails staying intact.*
