## 1. Reframing (from master §B3)

Old vision (federated learning + AI DAO + token economy) is deprecated. New framing =
**sovereignty**: who owns the agent, where it runs, who it depends on. Crypto rails matter
only where they genuinely fit (agent-to-agent micropayments) — not as the headline.

## 2. The 2026 landscape (none own "sovereign agent" framing yet)

| Player | Layer | Position (2026) |
|---|---|---|
| **Bittensor (TAO)** | intelligence market | ~$2.4-3.4B mcap, 64 active subnets, live economic activity; validators score models (trust-in-incentives) |
| **Gensyn** | verifiable compute | proof system mathematically certifies compute (no need to trust validators); largely pre-mainnet as of 2026 |
| **Ritual** | sovereign execution layer | embeds AI models into blockchain execution; modular orchestration, not a cloud replacement |
| **Render (RNDR) / Akash (AKT)** | DePIN compute | aggregate idle GPUs; Akash ML GPU mainnet for training/inference |
| **Fetch.ai / ASI Alliance, Virtuals Protocol** | agent economies | tokenized agents that earn/transact/coordinate on-chain; micropayments |

**Read**: the *compute* (Akash/Render/Gensyn) and *intelligence-market* (Bittensor) layers are
crowded. The *agent economy* layer (Virtuals/Fetch) is forming. The **"sovereign agent for
governments/enterprises that won't depend on US Big Tech"** framing is still open — that's SentientNet's lane.

## 3. The four pillars vs reality

- **User-owned agents** (run on your infra) — closest to Ritual's sovereign-execution idea; differentiator is the *sovereignty* promise, not the chain.
- **Agent-to-agent micropayments** — the one place crypto rails clearly fit (Virtuals/FET prove demand).
- **Sovereign deployment** (Thai gov / SEA / EU, no US dependency) — geopolitical pull is real; this is the wedge.
- **Federated agent learning** — learn across orgs without centralizing data; overlaps blockchain-architect + governance KB.

## 4. Honest reality check (invoke before any token framing)

- This is the **furthest-out** project (3-5-10 yr). Requires Track A profitable AND NeurX + Regent established.
- Most "AI crypto" is hype-heavy; ask **"does it even need blockchain?"** (blockchain-consultant lens) before committing rails.
- Defensible angle is **sovereignty + federation for regulated/regional actors**, not competing with Bittensor on intelligence markets.
